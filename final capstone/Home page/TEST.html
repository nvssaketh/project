Farming Do's and Don'ts

Knowledge = More yield

Do's Don'ts Resources About Us Contact

Do's

  • Do rotate your crops regularly to maintain soil health.
  • Do use organic compost to enrich soil nutrients.
  • Do test your soil regularly to understand its needs.
  • Do practice integrated pest management to reduce chemical use.
  • Do ensure proper irrigation techniques to conserve water.

Don'ts

  • Don't overuse chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • Don't neglect the importance of soil pH balance.
  • Don't plant the same crop repeatedly on the same land.
  • Don't ignore the benefits of cover crops.
  • Don't forget to maintain your farming equipment.
